Welcome!

I am currently a postdoc in the laboratory of Dr. Victoria Orphan at California Institute of Technology. My current research focuses on developing hydrogel-based platforms for enrichment of microbes from deepsea sediments. Before the 2-minute move the current lab, I did my PhD work and a short postdoc under the advisement of Dr. Michael R. Hoffmann, where I developed simulation-guided 3D microfluidic tools for rapid, sensitive, and affordable environmental pathogen detection and single cell analysis.
